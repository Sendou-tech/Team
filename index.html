Ratan Tata

“ I don't believe in taking the right decisions. I take decisions and then make them right ”

Ratan Tata, born on December 28, 1937, is a prominent Indian industrialist and philanthropist. He served as the chairman of Tata Sons, the holding company of the Tata Group, from 1991 to 20121. Under his leadership, the Tata Group expanded significantly, acquiring global brands like Jaguar Land Rover and Tetley. Ratan Tata is known for his visionary leadership and commitment to corporate social responsibility. He has been awarded India's highest civilian honors, the Padma Vibhushan and the Padma Bhushan. Beyond business, he is also a noted philanthropist, contributing to various charitable causes through the Tata Trusts

A.P.J Abdul Kalam

“ Learning gives creativity, creativity leads to thinking, thinking provides knowledge, and knowledge makes you great. ”

Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam's life lessons emphasize dreaming big, perseverance, and humility. He believed in setting ambitious goals and working tirelessly towards them. Kalam championed the importance of education and continuous learning, inspiring millions through his own journey from a small town to the Presidency. He taught that failures are stepping stones to success, urging people to embrace challenges. His dedication to innovation, simplicity, and integrity serves as a timeless inspiration, reminding us to remain grounded and committed to our vision.

B.R. Ambedkar

“ If you want to make real progress in life, you must be willing to have your ideas challenged. ”

Dr. B.R. Ambedkar's life lessons emphasize equality, education, and resilience. He championed social justice, fighting against untouchability and advocating for the rights of marginalized communities. Ambedkar believed in the transformative power of education and worked tirelessly to ensure access for all. His perseverance in the face of adversity and his commitment to democracy and human rights serve as an enduring inspiration. Ambedkar's legacy teaches us the importance of standing up for justice, empowering oneself through knowledge, and never giving up despite obstacles.
